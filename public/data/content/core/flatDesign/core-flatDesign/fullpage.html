Visual Philosophy

01

Blocks & Typography

We discard volume and material realism. Instead, we rely on simple geometry, solid backgrounds, and strong contrast. The whole UI behaves like a well-composed information poster.

02

Hierarchy

Hierarchy is expressed through font size, weight, color value, and whitespace rather than shadows or bevels.

03

Zones

Large areas of flat color divide the page into zones. Primary hues carry key actions, light neutrals preserve breathing room.

Interaction & Mood

In the flat family, "material" is a single layer of solid color.

Interaction rules:

  • Color shifts, not movement.
  • Toggled fills for state changes.
  • Short timings.
  • No glows, no dropshadows.

"Buttons are simply colored rectangles, not sculpted objects."
