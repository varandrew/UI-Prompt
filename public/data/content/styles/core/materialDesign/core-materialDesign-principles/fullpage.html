Visual Philosophy

Paper Surfaces + Elevation

The interface is a world of abstract paper sheets. Depth is conveyed through shadows (elevation).

Material & Texture

Surfaces: White or light neutrals. No noise.

Colors: Bold Primary (Purple) and Secondary (Teal) accents.

Shape: Small, consistent corner radii (4px).

Text hierarchy uses opacity for clarity.

Overall Mood

Modern. Friendly. System-like.
We aren't creating a one-off website. We are building a coherent ecosystem where layers of well-ordered cards and comfortable spacing create an approachable, legible interface. It is tactile, but efficient.

High Elevation

Dialogs sit at the highest elevation, floating above all other content. The background is dimmed (scrim) to focus user attention.
