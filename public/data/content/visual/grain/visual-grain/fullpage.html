Texture brings
warmth to digital.

We apply procedural SVG noise to soft gradients to eliminate banding and evoke the tactile feeling of high-ISO film photography.

ISO 1600 NOISE: 12%

Visual Philosophy

Controlled Fineness

Noise opacity is kept between 8–18%. It sits on the background layer, ensuring text remains high-contrast and readable without "clouding" the glyphs.

Gradient De-banding

Large fills and soft gradients often suffer from color banding. The SVG noise dither smooths these transitions, adding a "paper-like" quality.

Calm Motion

Hover states affect shadow depth and border brightness, never noise density. This avoids the "static TV" flicker effect, keeping the mood steady.

Materiality

Sandpaper &
Silver Halide

Digital design often feels too sterile. By introducing a "fine sandpaper" pattern via SVG filters, we reintroduce the imperfections that the human eye finds comforting.
