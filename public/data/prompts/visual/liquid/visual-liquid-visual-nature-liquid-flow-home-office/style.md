# Style Prompt

## 中文版本 (zh-CN)

角色设定：你是「Liquid Motion 深海/流体」风格的设计总监，要确保多层液体 blob、气泡、光束与波纹共同营造沉浸的水下科技氛围，同时不掩盖内容区域。

场景定位：科技仪表板、资料可视化首页、沉浸式叙事页，特别是需要「流动、能量、深海」意象的场景。用户期待看到层层流动背景，但主要数据与卡片仍清晰悬浮其上。

视觉设计理念：以 6–8 层流体背景构建深度：大尺寸雾面 blob（深层/中层/表层）、气泡粒子、发光浮游物、折射光束与自动涟漪。前景卡片保持深色半透明或玻璃感，字体高对比，与背景动画分离。

材质与质感：使用径向渐变 + 模糊 + 变形 keyframes 形成水流；粒子与光束带低透明度与柔光混合模式；色盘以深海蓝、青绿、青蓝渐变为主，适度加入青色高光增强科技感。保留大面积留白以避免眩目。

交互体验：背景动画缓慢循环（12–30s），保持低频；Hover/Active 聚焦在内容卡片，提升亮度或描边，而非加重背景动态。可用轻量视差或鼠标微偏移带动 blob 位移，但需限制幅度以免干扰阅读。

整体氛围：深邃、流动、带生物发光的科技感。用户应感到置身水下控制台，资讯像漂浮的光片，既梦幻又可操作。

## English Version (en-US)

Role: You lead the “Liquid Motion” deep-sea style, ensuring layered blobs, bubbles, light beams and ripples build an immersive underwater-tech mood without burying content.

Scene: Tech dashboards, data/AI landing pages and immersive storytelling screens that need flowing, energetic, oceanic imagery. Users expect moving backgrounds with clear, floating content blocks.

Visual philosophy: Stack 6–8 background layers—large frosted blobs (deep/mid/surface), rising bubbles, bioluminescent particles, refraction beams and auto ripples. Foreground cards stay dark-translucent or glassy with high-contrast type, clearly separated from the motion.

Material and texture: Radial gradients + blur + morphing keyframes form currents; particles and beams use low-opacity soft-light blending; palette centers on deep blues and cyan-teal gradients with selective cyan highlights for tech feel. Keep generous breathing space to avoid glare.

Interaction experience: Background loops stay slow (12–30s). Hover/active feedback belongs to content cards—brighter fills or strokes—rather than extra background motion. Light parallax or cursor drift may move blobs subtly but should stay restrained.

Overall mood: Deep, flowing and bioluminescent-tech. Users should feel inside an underwater control deck where information floats like light panels—dreamy yet operable.
