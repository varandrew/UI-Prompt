# Style Prompt

## 中文版本 (zh-CN)

角色设定：你是把科幻 HUD 语言套用到「居家办公/生产力仪表板」的设计师，要在冷静科技感与日常可用性之间取得平衡，让工作模块看起来像个人舱控台。

场景定位：远端工作站、任务与里程碑跟踪、资源监控、通知中心。使用者期待看到 HUD 风格的网格、描边与光效，但同时能轻松阅读日程、任务和系统状态。

视觉设计理念：背景保持深色渐变 + 细网格；主模块（生产力图表、时间轴、系统环形图、任务列表）用半透明卡片与青色描边区隔。字体以等宽体呈现数据，标题可使用几何无衬线。状态色沿用 HUD 语言：正常青色、警告琥珀、错误红色。

材质与质感：卡片带轻微内发光与薄描边，切角或细分隔线强化科技感；背景粒子与扫描线降低透明度，避免干扰阅读。按钮与开关以矩形/胶囊形呈现，边框发光但保持克制。

交互体验：Hover 时卡片/按钮上浮 1–2px、描边亮度上升；Active 时轻微下沉并收敛光晕。图表可带缓慢脉冲或扫描动画（2–4s），但文字与表格保持稳定。整体动效 150–220ms，重视可用性。

整体氛围：沉稳的个人控制台，带科幻味但不压迫。使用者应感觉自己在「家用舱桥」里管理任务与资源，界面有光、有数据，但节奏柔和易读。

## English Version (en-US)

Role: You apply the sci-fi HUD language to a home-office/productivity dashboard, balancing cool tech cues with everyday usability so modules feel like a personal cockpit.

Scene: Remote workstations, milestone/task tracking, resource monitoring and notification hubs. Users want HUD grids, strokes and glows but still need relaxed readability for schedules and tasks.

Visual philosophy: Keep dark gradients with fine grids; primary modules (productivity charts, timelines, system rings, task lists) sit in translucent cards with cyan strokes. Use monospace for numbers and geometric sans for headings. Status colors follow HUD norms: cyan for normal, amber for warnings, red for errors.

Material and texture: Cards carry light inner glow and thin strokes, with clipped corners or fine dividers for tech flavor; particles and scanlines stay low-opacity to avoid noise. Buttons/toggles use rectangular or pill shapes with restrained glows.

Interaction experience: On hover, cards/buttons lift 1–2px and brighten strokes; on active, they press slightly and tighten glow. Charts may pulse or scan slowly (2–4s), while text/tables stay steady. Transitions live around 150–220ms to prioritize usability.

Overall mood: A calm personal cockpit—sci-fi flavored but not oppressive. Users should feel they’re running a “home bridge,” with illuminated data that remains clear and comfortable.
