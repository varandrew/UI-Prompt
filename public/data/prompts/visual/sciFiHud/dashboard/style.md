# Style Prompt

## 中文版本 (zh-CN)

角色设定：你是科幻 HUD 仪表板的首席设计师，要营造「军事级指挥中心 / 船舰控制台」的视觉。任务是保持高密度数据可读，同时展现雷达扫描、能量条、网格线与发光描边。

场景定位：系统监控、航天/防务/硬核科技仪表板、即时状态大屏。使用者期待看到深色基底、青色/绿色光效、萤幕网格、扫描扇形与脉冲指示灯。

视觉设计理念：背景为暗色渐变 + 细网格/扫描线；主区域使用分栏卡片与圆形雷达，搭配等宽字体的数据行。描边与分隔线用低透明度青色，关键指示灯采亮青/琥珀/红色状态色。层级靠亮度、描边强度、阴影来分隔，而非厚重色块。

材质与质感：玻璃面板 + 金属刻线的混合语言：边角可做切角，卡片带细描边与轻微内发光；背景星点或粒子低透明度铺陈科技感。图表使用线框/点阵风格，避免厚填充。

交互体验：Hover 时描边与光点加亮，卡片轻微上浮；Active 时按钮下沉并光晕收敛。雷达与指示灯持续以 2–6s 的平滑循环运动；过场动效保持 150–220ms，避免炫酷但不实用。

整体氛围：精密、冷静、战术感强。用户应觉得自己在操作高阶舰桥，信息密集但井然有序，光效克制且可信。

## English Version (en-US)

Role: You lead sci-fi HUD dashboards that feel like a command bridge—tactical, data-dense and precise, with radar sweeps, energy bars, grids and glowing strokes.

Scene: System monitoring, aerospace/defense/hard-tech control panels and real-time status walls. Users expect dark bases, cyan/green glows, screen grids, scanning sectors and pulsing status lights.

Visual philosophy: Use dark gradients plus fine grids/scanlines; structure content into columns/cards and a central radar; data uses monospace lines. Strokes and dividers sit in low-alpha cyan, with status colors in bright cyan/amber/red. Hierarchy relies on brightness, stroke weight and shadow, not heavy fills.

Material and texture: A mix of glass panels and metal engravings: clipped corners, thin strokes, gentle inner glow; sparse starfield/particle layers for subtle tech ambience. Charts favor wireframes/dots over thick fills.

Interaction experience: On hover, strokes and indicators brighten and cards lift slightly; on active, buttons press down and glows tighten. Radar and indicators animate in 2–6s smooth loops; transitions stay at 150–220ms to stay functional over flashy.

Overall mood: Precise, calm and tactical. Users should feel on a high-end bridge—information is dense but orderly, and lighting is disciplined enough to trust.
