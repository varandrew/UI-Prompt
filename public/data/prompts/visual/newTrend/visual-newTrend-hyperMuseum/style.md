# Style Prompt

## 中文版本 (zh-CN)

角色设定：你要为「HYPER MUSEUM / 极繁艺术节」这类展演页设计风格卡，延续 New Trend 的极繁语言，但聚焦于展馆/艺术节场景：大量贴纸、舞台光、跑马灯、Bento Grid、艺术家卡片与售票 CTA。

场景定位：艺术节、音乐节、装置展的主宣传页或售票页。访客期待高饱和撞色、粗描边、浮动几何与贴纸，并能快速找到档期、阵容、购票入口。

视觉设计理念：用亮底（黄/粉/蓝）+ 粗黑描边作为舞台；主标语使用超粗字重与轮廓描边；跑马灯贯穿顶部或分隔区块；Bento Grid 装载展览亮点、艺术家卡片与票务；浮动形状与贴纸作为背景点缀，保持「展场飘浮物」的趣味。

材质与质感：纸质贴纸 + 厚描边 + 平移硬阴影；高饱和双色渐变可用于 spotlight 区块；加入少量噪点和棋盘格线框提升粗糙度。彩色圆形/方形浮块带 2–4px 边框，动画轻微漂移。

交互体验：Hover 时卡片旋转 1–2° 并上浮、阴影加深；按钮像贴纸被轻撕起，亮度与饱和度上升；跑马灯线性连续，不要突然停顿。动效节奏 180–260ms，保持演出般的活力但让 CTA 清晰可点。

整体氛围：舞台、展演、狂欢。用户进来会被色彩与元素轰炸，但能立即找到「档期/阵容/购票」这些关键讯息，并感到这是同一系列的极繁展览页。

## English Version (en-US)

Role: You design the style card for a “HYPER MUSEUM / maximalist festival” page, extending the New Trend language but tailored to exhibitions: stickers, stage lights, marquees, bento grids, artist cards and ticket CTAs.

Scene: Festival or exhibition promo/sales pages. Visitors expect saturated clashes, thick strokes, floating shapes and stickers, while quickly finding dates, lineup and ticket entry points.

Visual philosophy: Build a stage with bright bases (yellow/pink/blue) and bold black strokes; headlines use ultra-heavy weights with outlined letters; marquees split sections; the bento grid houses highlights, artist cards and ticket tiles. Floating shapes and stickers sit behind content to mimic “objects in the hall.”

Material and texture: Paper stickers with 2–4px strokes and hard-offset shadows; saturated dual-tone gradients for spotlight areas; light grain and checker frames for grit. Colored blobs or squares carry borders and drift subtly.

Interaction experience: On hover, cards tilt 1–2° and lift with deeper shadows; buttons behave like peeled stickers with brighter fill; marquees scroll linearly without abrupt stops. Motion sits around 180–260ms to keep showtime energy while keeping CTAs obvious.

Overall mood: Stage-like, celebratory and maximal. Users should be dazzled by color but still spot “dates/lineup/tickets” immediately, feeling it clearly belongs to the same maximalist series.
