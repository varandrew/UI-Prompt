# Custom Prompt

## 中文版本 (zh-CN)

设计亲自然（Biophilic）风格界面，将自然元素融入设计，创造宁静舒适的用户体验。

**核心视觉特征**：大面积留白、柔和有机曲线、绿植插画点缀、自然材质纹理、温暖大地色系、柔和阴影效果。

**色彩系统**：绿色调（#22c55e, #16a34a, #15803d）、土色调（#92400e, #78350f）、天空蓝（#0ea5e9, #0284c7）、米色/奶油色（#fef3c7, #fef08a）、深棕绿文字（#1a4d2e, #064e3b）。背景：浅米色 #fefce8 或淡绿 #f0fdf4。

**布局与元素**：柔和圆角（16-24px）、有机形状卡片、绿植装饰插画、木质纹理背景、自然图标（叶子、树木、水滴）、宽松间距营造呼吸感、流体布局模拟自然流动。

**动画**：缓慢柔和过渡（400-600ms ease-out）、轻微浮动效果模拟植物摇曳、淡入淡出动画、避免突兀快速动画。

**典型应用**：健康/健身应用、冥想/正念工具、环保/可持续发展网站、有机食品/农场、瑜伽/自然疗法、园艺/植物商店。

## English Version (en-US)

Design biophilic style interfaces that incorporate natural elements, creating serene and comfortable user experiences.

**Core Visual Characteristics**: Ample whitespace, soft organic curves, plant illustration accents, natural material textures, warm earth tone palette, soft shadow effects.

**Color System**: Green tones (#22c55e, #16a34a, #15803d), earth tones (#92400e, #78350f), sky blue (#0ea5e9, #0284c7), beige/cream (#fef3c7, #fef08a), deep brown-green text (#1a4d2e, #064e3b). Backgrounds: Light beige #fefce8 or pale green #f0fdf4.

**Layout & Elements**: Soft corners (16-24px), organic-shaped cards, plant decorative illustrations, wood texture backgrounds, natural icons (leaves, trees, water drops), spacious gaps for breathing room, fluid layouts mimicking natural flow.

**Animation**: Slow gentle transitions (400-600ms ease-out), slight floating effects simulating plant swaying, fade in/out animations, avoid abrupt fast animations.

**Typical Applications**: Health/fitness apps, meditation/mindfulness tools, eco/sustainability websites, organic food/farms, yoga/natural therapy, gardening/plant shops.
